LG Prada

A true rival to the iPhone the super slim LG Prada has a stunning touchscreen with high level features including digital camera and MP3 player.

Our LG Prada Review

The LG KE850 Prada phone is without doubt the best looking mobile phone to be released so far this year. With its stunning slim good looks and minimalist touch sensitive screen the Prada Phone will be one of the top selling high end phones of 2007.

There is no doubt that the Prada Phone will be in competition with handsets like the iPhone rather than the LG Chocolate as it will be more expensive than standard handsets.

At only 12mm thin and weighing just 85 grams the Prada is a very slim phone but still has room for a huge 3 inch 240x 400 pixel colour touch screen.

The user interface on the LG Prada is very intuitive and stylish (see video) and is one of the outstanding features on the Prada phone. A built in 2 megapixel digital camera takes high quality snaps thanks to the Schneider Kreuznach lens and LED flash.

Videos and photos can be played backing widescreen format on the large screen which also works very well for viewing web pages and emails over the fast EDGE internet connection.

LG have also included an FM radio with MP3 player expandable to 1GB thanks to a MicroSD memory card as well as Bluetooth and 8MB of internal memory.

The LG Prada also has a few good business functions including PowerPoint, Microsoft Excel, Word and Adobe Acrobat for pdf files. Batter life is 300 hours standby with 3 hours talk time.

You really have to see the LG Prada phone in the flesh to see how stunning it is. Once you see it you will want one!
