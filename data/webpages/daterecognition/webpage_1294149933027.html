How would you describe what you did at scad

My major was Interior Design, so I spent a lot of time doing Studio work in AutoCAD and Photoshop. My minor was Architectural History, which gives me a broader perspective on Interiors and helped me understand the connection between the shell and the interior better than solely focusing on Interior Design.

I was also able to attend the Off-Campus Program in Italy, Summer 2006, where I focused on Architectural and Art history. My courses were "Renaissance and Baroque Architecture" and "Reading Urban Form." It was an amazing first-hand experience that greatly influenced my work....
