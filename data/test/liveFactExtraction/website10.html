Asus Eee PC 1000

by Jin on June 2, 2008

in Notebook PC

Asus Eee PC 1000
Display Size: 10.2"
CPU: Atom
OS: Linux or Windows XP Home
Card Reader: MMC, SD/SDHC
RAM: Up to 2GB DDR2
Storage:

  • 40GB SSD (Eee PC 1000, Linux)
  • 80GB HD (Eee PC 1000H - XP or Linux)

Audio: HD Audio, Stereo Speakers, Array Microphone
Communication: 10/100 Ethernet, 802.11b/g/n, Bluetooth
Keyboard: 92% full size
Webcam: 1.3MP
Battery: 3.2 to 5 hours New 6-cell goes 7 1/2 hours (7.8 hours for 901, 7 hours for 1000H [HDD model])
Weight: 1.45kg

Update 2008.06.13 5:14PM PST
Engadget is reporting that the Eee PC 1000 with either Linux or Windows XP will be running at $699 and the Eee PC 1000H version with the HDD will run $649. There is also an video unboxing of the Eee PC 1000H thanks to TweakTown (via Engadget).

Update 2008.06.11 7:38AM PST
Engadget is reporting that the Eee PC 1000H is now finally available. In Taiwan. The price is right at US$625 (NT$18,988).

Blogeee.net has some pictures up of the Eee PC 1000, which will be officially unveiled tomorrow at Computex. The bigger LCD and bigger keyboard should be welcome by those who are bigger but want to tote around a very small notebook PC.

But... the Eee PC notebook PCs are getting bigger and bigger. And, unfortunately, more expensive. Like I said in previous posts I hope they don't lose that "small is better" and "cheaper is better" focus. But I think they will lose it. Ultimately, I see a 15.4" Eee PC that has no distinction from other notebook PC brands.

Update 2008.06.02 10:49PM PST
Engadget has some prices for us:

  • Eee PC 901: NT$16,988, ~US$559
  • Eee PC 1000(H): NT$18,988, ~US$625
  • Eee PC 1000: NT$19,988, ~US$658

These prices are still mighty cheap! I hope it stays that way once it crosses the Pacific!

Update 2008.06.03 10:35PM PST
The Eee PC 1000 is finally officially revealed. Here is video of the event at Computex 2008.

Source: Engadget (1, 2, 3), Slash Gear
