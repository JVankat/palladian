Forza Motorsport 2's
Official Car List

 

4.19.07 // 09:00

 

By: Che Chou

 

Cars. You love 'em, we've got 'em. It's been a long time building, but we're proud to present to you now the complete Forza Motorsport 2 car list -- 310 gorgeous rides you'll be able to tune, mod, upgrade, paint, customize, auction, and completely make your own. Keep in mind that among this list are 7 cars which carry a special status, be they only cars you can acquire if you preorder the game, or buy the Forza Motorsport 2 Collector's Edition... or the controversial unicorn cars you can only get from Turn 10 as we release them in very limited supplies in our online Auction House.

 

Forza Motorsport 2 is coming soon. To help make the wait a little easier, be sure to check out the latest screenshots in our Media section!
