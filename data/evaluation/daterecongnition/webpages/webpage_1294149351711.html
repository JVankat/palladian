Austin’s largest real estate networking and investing club! The Austin RENC is comprised of several hundred of Austin's most experienced real estate investors as well as a constant influx of new investors.  We typically have 70-100 members and guests at our monthly meetings.  Our focus is on Central Texas real estate investing.  After all, Real Estate markets are local and you need a club that has a local focus.


Austin RENC pledges to bring you speakers and discussion topics that are relevant to building the investment knowledge you need to be successful in Texas.  We believe in education and getting our membership the most accurate and up-to-date information about what's going on in Texas and what's working in TODAY'S real estate market.  Our presenters are highly vetted so you only see the best people speaking directly about topics that affect your business in Texas.  We are not a multi-level marketing group, never have been, never will be.  We have a stringent focus on networking, education, investment strategies, deal pitching, and knowledge sharing, not on selling products or services.  These are just a few things that differentiate us and make our group a stand-out for both accomplished and new real estate investors.


To understand the language of real estate, it helps to network with others who are fluent in Real Estate Investing. Austin RENC members are a diverse group of aspiring and accomplished real estate investors of varying levels. We hope you'll join us at our next meeting.
