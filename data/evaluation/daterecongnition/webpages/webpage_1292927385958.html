threefold architects was formed in 2004 to make innovative, exciting architecture that enhances it’s environment and improves the lives of the people who use and experience it. We treat each project as an opportunity to find unique solutions that meet the specific requirements of the client, whilst constantly striving to exceed their expectations. We are a practice of qualified registered architects that is strongly committed to quality, sustainability, delivery, and innovation. Our goal is to create excellent design married with the highest level of service in every project we undertake. We have an intentionally varied client base currently including a mixture of residential, commercial and arts clients, with work ranging from new build projects to the restoration and conversion of Listed Buildings. We believe strongly in the importance of design quality at both macro and micro levels, with all aspects of our projects receiving equal care, from the smallest detail to the overall strategy. Each project is embarked upon with a fresh approach where every scheme grows from and responds to the context of the site, the budget and the needs of the user. We are constantly enhancing our knowledge of new materials, technologies and construction techniques to broaden our palette to meet these new challenges.
