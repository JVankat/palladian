Why I am a Marauder

I am part of the Moore�s Marauders because of the impact this successful mission has on the lives of family members of this country�s heroes. Because of the sacrifices that these lost souls have made for us their posterity, I will do all in my power to bring these heroes home.

Lon Lease & Family

The effort, the resources, the cost, to recover these warriors is small in comparison to the sacrifices these men have made.

My career as an Air Force pilot and flight safety investigator was but preparation for this mission of investigation and identification of MIA throughout the world. As I flew to many WWII sites throughout my career, I couldn�t help but imagine the sacrifices and the people who preceded me into places like Tinian, Iwo Jima, Subic Bay, Clark AB, etc.

I come from a long line of military members including a WWII Seabee and an Army Air Corps pilot. I know personally the cost to families for service in the military. While there are many historical, military based organizations that I have encountered, this is unique in that this one puts action and purpose to historical significance. I am proud to be a part of such a fine collection of professional, purpose driven comrades with such a high calling.
