Netanyahu and Abbas have agreed to continue talks in two weeks.




Idle brained Bubbles in the White House on other side of sea has hedged his bet on the wrong side again.  Obama has hedged that Mahmoud Abbas will make a deal.  However there's a dose of reality therapy to educate the delusional with:  Regardless of what Palestine's leaders say, Hamas will use violence to attempt sabotaging any deal. 

There's a valid reason why Israel is unwilling to divide their capital again, something Obama and Abbas is hoping to become a topic to broach.  It is, and shall be off limits. 

Don't these impostors realise that Jerusalem is an Israeli city.  When the Jordanians occupied parts of the Old City for roughly two decades, they had barred Israeli Jews and Christians from their holy places.  Today, the Palestinians are as equally unyielding in their demands that portions of Jerusalem becomes their capital. 

Not only do the Palestinians want an immediate stop to all settlement building, they aspire to have some of the land in which these housing plans are being constructed on, to be surrendered to them, as portions of their parts of the Old City.  I say no way to either of these possibilities.

There should be no stop to settlement building, because it is Israel's land in the first place.  Technically, this is an unreasonable request. The israelis never made a settlement freeze as a stipulation for talks in the past.  Why should they now? 

The Palestinian obnoxiousness doesn't stop there:  Now Hamas want Israel to forgo these settlements just for opportunity to get the Palestinians to negotiate.  

Just what are the Palestinians giving up?  That question is not being asked by those sock puppets and media's mental midgets.  Let Hamas offer to give up the "right of return" of Palestine refugees for a cessation of settlement construction. 

We all know any concession by the Palestinians isn't going to happen. That's because their government is operated by terrorist thugs. 

Now what does this reveal?  The old adage of negotiating with terrorists ring true again.  Don't negotiate anything with these thugs.
