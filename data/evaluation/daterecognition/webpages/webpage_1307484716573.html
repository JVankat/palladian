Support Us

Individual Support: Membership Matters

You can pledge by calling 501-569-8485 or 1-800-952-2528 or using our online pledge form.

Individual contributions provide the largest portion of our annual funding, accounting for roughly 40% of our operating budget. We could not continue to provide the programming and services our listeners have come to expect without the continued financial support of individuals like you!

More than 70,000 persons listen to KUAR and KLRE each week, but less than 4,000 of them financially support the stations. That's less than 6% of our weekly audience.  Imagine the services and programming we could provide if we increased that number to just 10%!

Become a member now, renew your membership, or give an additional gift to ensure the quality of programming on which you rely in Central Arkansas. Each and every gift, regardless of amount, is an important part of our funding. And of course, your gift is tax deductible. We appreciate you taking the time to make your pledge!

Corporate Support: Become an Underwriter

An important contribution to quality radio is made by foundations, corporations and businesses that provide funds to underwrite the presentation of a program broadcast on KUAR and KLRE. 88% of NPR listeners say their opinion of a company is more positive when they discover the company supports public radio.

Underwriters can target a specific audience by choosing the programming during which the underwriting message will air.

Call us at 501-569-8485 to find out more, or e-mail Laurie Pierce at laurie@kuar.org.
