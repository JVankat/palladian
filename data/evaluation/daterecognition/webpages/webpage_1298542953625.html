One Place. One Purpose.

Home

 We’re driven every day, in everything we do, to help you reach your true potential. That’s why we designed one place that offers comprehensive rehabilitation and transitional care exclusively focused on helping you meet each important milestone in your recovery process. You’ll discover state-of-the-art technology, advanced therapies and wellness environments, and an unparalleled level of care and encouragement — all under one roof.

Our specialized team of rehab physicians, clinicians, nurses, and therapists are dedicated to one thing — enabling you to reclaim your independence and your life.

  • A 62-bed Rehabilitation Center including an 11-bed secured Brain Injury Unit 
  • A 50-bed Transitional Care Center for skilled care
  • Spacious private rooms with large private bathrooms and flat screen televisions
  • State-of-the-art rehabilitation technology 
  • Aquatic therapy pool
  • On-site pharmacy, laboratory, and radiological services
  • Outdoor therapeutic garden
  • Comfortable lounges with flat screen televisions, computers, and free internet access
  • Plenty of visiting areas for patients, friends, and families
  • Café for family dining
  • Easy access and free parking
