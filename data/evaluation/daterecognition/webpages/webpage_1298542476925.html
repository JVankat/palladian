Confluence plugins and extensionsHundreds of ways to extend Confluence

Confluence's plugin system makes it easy to customise and extend Confluence for your specific intranet, technical writing and knowledge management needs. The Atlassian Plugin Exchange is the largest plugin ecosystem of any commercial enterprise wiki platform.

We wanted something we could easily extend. We'd used other wikis in the past with varying degrees of success. This time we wanted something with permissioning, and something that was polished and secure. Confluence met all of these needs. — Turadg Aleahmad of TELS Center, University of California at Berkeley

Plugins and extensions for everything!Search the Atlassian Plugin Exchange »

Popular

TaskDock

Full-loop task management - Assign, track, and complete tasks within Confluence and email.

Balsamiq

Create UI wireframes and website mockups within a page using drag-and-drop.

Gliffy

Create flow charts, diagrams and floorplans within a page using drag-and-drop.

Scroll

Collaborate and author documentation in Confluence, then export to a properly formatted and styled Word document.

Ad-hoc Workflows

Manage custom workflows and content approvals while keeping the open wiki philosophy.

Clients and Themes

RefinedWiki Theme

Turn your wiki into an intranet with a whole new interface which increases usability of Confluence with improved navigation.

Zen Foundation

Skins and themes for Confluence providing easy branding, rich layouts, and beautiful blogging.

ConfluenceFx

A desktop client for Confluence that lets you create content, receive wiki notifications and do much, much more.

Mini Confluence

A native app for iPhones and Androids providing access to and use of Confluence from your mobile device.

Themebuilder

Build your own theme - easily customise logo banners, menu navigation, style sheets and more.

Content Collaboration

Google Calendar

Embeds a dynamically updated Google calendar into any page.

Community Bubbles

Adds forum and community management features to Confluence.

Copy Partial Space

Reorganise and manage content by copy any branch of a space into a new space.

Numbered Headings

Customise the formatting of headings to display number formats including decimal and roman.

Page Templates

Import bundles of page templates to help users get started and spur adoption.

Connectors

SharePoint Connector

Combine Confluence's easy-to-edit wiki with the document management strengths of SharePoint.

Google Docs

Embed, access, and navigate your Google Docs file directory in Confluence.

Salesforce.com

Insert filtered lists of Salesforce contacts, accounts, leads and other entities in Confluence.

Alfresco Connector

List, access, edit, and/or delete Alfresco documents from within Confluence.

SQL Plugin

Create and display dynamic reports from any database in your enterprise.

Develop your own plugin

Have an idea for a plugin? With the Atlassian Plugin SDK you can easily develop your own plugin and have it listed on the Plugin Exchange.

  • We've put together detailed instructions on how to get started developing your own plugin.
  • Collaborate with other developers through our thriving developer community forum system.
  • Ready to release? Need somewhere to store your code? No worries. We can set you up with a free subversion server, JIRA, FishEye, Bamboo and Crucible project space so you can better manage your project.
