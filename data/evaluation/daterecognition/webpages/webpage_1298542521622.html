Job Details

Marketing Executive

Permanent, South London
Up to £25,000 per annum
Ref: DG007544

We are looking for an experienced Marketing Executive to work for a market leading organisation servicing the travel industry.

Working within the marketing team, you will be responsible for executing direct marketing campaigns across offline and online channels to audiences within the UK and EMEA.  Your online responsibilities will include updating web pages using a Content Management system, using Dreamweaver to amend HTML creative collateral, setting up URLs and new landing pages to support online acquisition activity and supporting PPC and SEM activity.  Additionally, you will be required to make amendments to images using Photoshop for offline collateral and be responsible for broadcasting email campaigns.

You will be an experienced Marketing Executive who has worked within a digital marketing role.  It is essential that you have experience of analysing and reporting on marketing activity and have worked to tight deadlines whilst managing simultaneous projects.  You will have excellent IT skills, including Dreamweaver, Photoshop and experience of using a Content Management system as well as the full MS Office suite.

This is a permanent role to start ASAP based in South London and is paying up to £25,000 per annum.

Due to the high number of applications for this role, we will be able to respond to successful applicants only.
