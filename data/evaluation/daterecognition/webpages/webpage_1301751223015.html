PHOTO: Friday night accident

September 18, 2010 6:13 PM
JOURNAL-COURIER

Vehicles driven by Debra McLeese, 57, of 17 Arbor Drive and Evan Scott, 19, of 136 Pine St. collided about 8:15 p.m. Friday at South Westgate and Morton avenues.

Both vehicles were driving east on Morton Avenue, McLeese in the outside lane and Scott in the inside lane. McLeese changed lanes in front of Scott, causing a rear-end collision. Scott’s vehicle spun out and rolled over.

McLeese was transported to Passavant Area Hospital where she was treated and released.

She will be cited on a charge of driving in the wrong lane.
