OPB In-Kind Food Donations

Please read the instructions at left and complete this form
If you are interested in participating in OPB's In-Kind Food Donor Program, please fill out the form at right.
Please note: Donating a dessert or dinner during a Membership Drive is the equivalent of 40 to 60 servings total.
For the last questions, please indicate the level at which your business wishes to be involved, and the platform (Radio or TV) you wish to donate for:
  • Level 1: I am willing to contribute/donate to all 7 Radio & TV Membership Drives and the occasional OPB event (trainings, Thank-a-Member Day, other one-day events).
  • Level 2: I am willing to contribute/donate to 4 Membership Drives per year and the occasional OPB event (trainings, Thank-a-Member Day, other one-day events).
  • Level 3: I am willing to contribute/donate to 2 to 4 Membership Drives per year.
  • Level 4: I am willing to contribute/donate to 1 to 2 OPB Membership Drives per year.
