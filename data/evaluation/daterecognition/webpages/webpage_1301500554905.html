Villa Sarita is a superbly positioned 3 bedroom villa with air conditioned/heated bedrooms and heated pool, with panoramic sea views. A popular villa to rent, a short walk from two picture-postcard cove beaches, each with a snack bar/restaurant.
 

VILLA LOCATION

This holiday villa to hire is near the Sao Rafael villa estate along the Sao Rafael coast, 2 miles west of Albufeira. This Algarve villa commands uninterrupted sea views, and is 880 yards from two cove beaches, each with a snack bar. Two restaurants and a mini-market are also within walking distance of this Algarve villa.
 

VILLA ACCOMMODATION
Villa Sarita is a bungalow-style villa with air conditioned/heated bedrooms in an excellent location, alongside a small lane running parallel to the sea, just a little way inland. Entry from the lane is up a sloping driveway to the side of this holiday rental with a private car port and space for two cars. The entrance door to this Algarve villa is on the same side. ...    [More]
The lounge/dining room is comfortably furnished. Doors from here open onto a small shaded terrace and directly to the south-facing pool area with a good-size private pool and superb open sea views. The pool is heated at no extra cost. The villa's kitchen opens to a shaded eating area, with an attractively tiled Portuguese-style table and bench seating, together with a barbecue for outside eating. There are two twins and a double bedroom at this Portuguese villa; the double bedroom and one of the twins have exterior doors opening directly to the pool terrace, facing the sea. The second twin bedroom opens to the side of the villa. There are two bathrooms. All bedrooms at this holiday villa are air conditioned/heated at no extra cost.
