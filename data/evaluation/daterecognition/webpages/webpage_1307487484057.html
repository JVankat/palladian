Firewall and Intrusion Prevention System Testing Service

Auditing the deployment, configuration and capabilities of your network security defences

Your network security defenses are constantly evolving to defend against the increasing level of threat sophistication used by the latest attacks and exploits.


The task of auditing and validating your security infrastructure while conforming to security compliance standards is becoming increasingly difficult, time consuming and expensive to accomplish. Without the ability to prove the effectiveness of your security infrastructure your business might be exposed to unnecessary risk.

Overview 

Auditing and validating the effectiveness of your network security defenses can be a complex and time consuming task. How do you quickly and easily prove your network security’s ability to protect you against the latest threats and security evasion techniques without creating a mountain of administrative complexity?


How do you effectively audit and prove your network security’s communication policy for regulatory compliance? Our service provides a unique industry approved solution to answer these questions and to maintain the integrity and effectiveness of your security infrastructure on an ongoing basis.

How we can help 

We will undertake a thorough and vendor independent assessment of your network security infrastructures capabilities to make sure your security policies are enforceable and that you are protected against the latest attacks and security evasion techniques. Our security specialists will investigate how vulnerable your business is to malicious intrusion, attack, and security evasion and then help you improve your security policy. Our vendor independence means you can count on us to provide a completely objective assessment. The next step is not to rip and replace, but to begin enhancing your existing infrastructure to maintain maximum protection against the latest threats and evasion techniques.

Our service

Our service provides a unique and industry approved methodology that not only assures accuracy but also versatility.

We will effectively assess the capabilities of all non-proxy based packet filtering devices including IDS/IPS systems, UTM devices, switches, routers and application layer firewalls.  During this assessment we will audit your network security infrastructures ability to recognize and mitigate thousands of real word exploits, attacks and security evasion techniques.

We ensure that the assessment of your network security infrastructure is conducted in a safe, controlled and repeatable manor, even in a live production environment.
After effectively “score carding” your security infrastructures capabilities, our highly trained professionals will assist you in enhancing your security policy to maximize your networks defensive capabilities.

Our technology

Our advanced assessment architecture is built upon Karalon Technologies “Traffic IQ Professional” product suite, an industry approved platform for the assessment of network security devices.


Our service enables us to provide you with the highest standards of analysis, resulting in clear and accurate reports detailing thecapabilities and effectiveness of your network security defences.
