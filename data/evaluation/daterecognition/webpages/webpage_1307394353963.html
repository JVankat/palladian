05.04.11 | Barn Owl Play The Captains Rest, Glasgow in April


The Radio Magnetic blog brings you the latest gig, festival and music news from throughout the UK and worldwide.

San Francisco duo, Barn Owl make the sort of cavernous, sandblasted-psych that would serve as the perfect soundtrack to watching a sun go supernova, and if that’s your bag then it would be in your interests to get along to the Captains Rest, Glasgow on Saturday 30 April to see them shake that venues foundations.

The bands style has been likened to Ennio Morricone, and even Sunn O))), but they are more meditation than metal. The band has support from kindred spirit Jefre Cantu-Ledesma, member of The Alps, and co-founder of the Root Strata label, as well as Heather-Leigh Murray (Charalambides, Jailbreak, Volcanic Tongue) and Hivver (Joe Quimby of Remember Remember/ Take a Worm For a Walk Week).

And as if that wasn’t enough, John Petrie from Radio Magnetic‘s very own Broken Ear podcast will be spinning tunes upstairs. This promises to be a great evening!
