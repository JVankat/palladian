Welcome to Ess-Food SAS

Ess-Food SAS, based in Orléans (45), sell pork and beef meat from Danish Crown. Furthermore we sell pork and beef meat of European origin for Europe, Eastern countries, Russia, South Africa, Asia….

Ess-Food is supported by its flexible cutting plant Desfis in Bonnetable (72) that undisputedly contributes to the group’s success with a taylor-made production for the French industry and for Ess-Food to export.


Moreover, our beef unit Raynal Petersen in Bernay (27) is exclusively meeting the French market’s demands in both Danish and non-Danish fresh and frozen beef products.
