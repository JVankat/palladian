SEMINAR ON ISSUES REGARDING STRUGGLE AGAINST TERROR STARTED IN YEREVAN

July 24, 2002 - 18:49 AMT
PanARMENIAN.Net - Today the seminar on the issues regarding the struggle against terror organized by the OSCE representative office in Armenia and the RA MFA started in Yerevan. Speaking at the seminar opening ceremony, OSCE representative office head Roy Reeve stated that within the framework of the seminar, the issues on prevention of document falsification, securing of visas and identity cards would be discussed. According to him, the practical examples of the struggle against the falsification of documents in the USA, the Great Britain and Slovenia would be considered. "We try to guarantee the conditions for the exchange of experience among the professionals and hope that the foreign experience would be useful for Armenia", Reeve noted.
