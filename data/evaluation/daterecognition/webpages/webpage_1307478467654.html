Welcome to the Avondale (Sr.) High School Graduating Class Of 1976 OFFICAL WEB SITE.

(The Avondale School District is located in the City of Auburn Hills, Michigan USA / formerly the unincorporated village of Auburn Heights in Pontiac Township).

More content and features will be added in the days and months ahead.

Also created was a page of Local History Photos.

The links to these pages can be found on the left side of this page below the link to our 2011 Reunion information.

These pages can be accessed by all registered website members.

A GREAT BIG THANK YOU TO THE 101 CLASSMATES AND FACULTY WHO REGISTERED HERE AT OUR NEW WEBSITE (AS OF WEDNESDAY 6-1-11).

A SPECIAL WELCOME TO OUR LATEST NEW MEMBERS

TED WILLIAMS

&

  PATTI LEE (REDPATH)

 

PLEASE NOTE THAT YOU ARE WELCOME TO JOIN THE SITE AS A MEMBER EVEN IF YOU WERE NOT A GRADUATE OF OUR CLASS. THIS INCLUDES TEACHERS, COACHES, COUNSELORS, OTHER FACULTY AND GRADUATES OF OTHER AVONDALE CLASS YEARS.

FRIENDS OF CLASSMATES ARE ALSO WELCOME AND CAN REGISTER AS "GUESTS".

SIMPLY CLICK ON THE "CONTACT US" LINK AT THE TOP OF THIS PAGE TO SEND A REQUEST TO HAVE YOUR NAME ADDED TO THE "MEMBER PROFILES" LIST. TELL US YOUR FIRST AND LAST NAME (INCLUDE THE NAME YOU WENT BY IN SCHOOL IF APPLICABLE - FOR EXAMPLE: MAIDEN NAME), YOUR E-MAIL ADDRESS AND/OR "SNAIL MAIL" ADDRESS AND YEAR OF GRADUATION IF YOU WERE AN AVONDALE GRADUATE.

THEN, UPON APPROVAL, YOUR NAME WILL BE ADDED TO THE "MEMBER PROFILES" PAGE. IF YOU PROVIDED US WITH A VALID E-MAIL ADDRESS, YOU WILL RECEIVE AN E-MAIL REPLY WITH INSTRUCTIONS ON HOW TO  COMPLETE YOU REGISTRATION TO JOIN AS A MEMBER OF THIS WEBSITE. 

Thank you for your interest in the new Class of '76 website.

The Avondale Class of '76 Reunion Committee is:

Mary Kamego, Terry "Da Man" Buday, Brian "Cheech" Churchill, David Michael Bland and Craig Pretznow (Web Site Administrator)
