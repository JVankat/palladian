Welcome to Sagebrush.

A holistic, residential center for substance abuse treatment.


Located in Northern Virginia outside Washington, DC, Sagebrush is a holistic residential substance abuse treatment center for adult men and women.

With its unique combination of innovative and traditional therapies, Sagebrush brings to greater Washington DC a private, individualized and exceptional program.

The center’s integrative approach to addiction treatment merges the twelve-step model of recovery with a customized treatment program designed to identify and eliminate the root causes of addiction.


What distinguishes Sagebrush is our small, individualized, high-quality clinical program that creates an environment ideal for beginning the journey of recovery. Sagebrush is committed to maintaining a sacred place that is intimate and secure, where the transformative work of recovery can begin.

The facility is spacious, peaceful and beautiful. Sagebrush provides a sanctuary where our clients can let down their walls, open their hearts and learn ways to find meaning again.

At Sagebrush, each personalized program focuses on mental, physical and spiritual well-being. The work is to facilitate authentic change in a compassionate and non-judgmental environment. Those who come here will be taught how to reconnect with their true selves.

Our expert staff help you to dissolve the pain and obstacles that have kept you from living the life that was meant -- for you.
