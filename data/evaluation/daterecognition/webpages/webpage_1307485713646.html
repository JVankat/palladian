Housing-complex violence in police spotlight

End of Supporting Story Content

Back to accessibility links

Beginning of Story Content

A 25-year-old man has been charged with manslaughter in connection with a stabbing death at a government housing complex on April 18, Winnipeg police say.

Ricky Martin Lathlin, 34, died after being attacked at the Gilbert Park housing complex in the city's Shaughnessy Heights neighbourhood. Police arrived around 5 a.m. after residents called in complaints of people roaming the area armed with baseball bats and a machete.

Officers found Lathlin outside one of the residences. He was rushed to hospital in critical condition but succumbed to his injuries.

Police said Jonathan Boyer, 25, of Winnipeg was arrested and charged Thursday.

They said another man, aged 26, was arrested on Friday and was being held in custody.

There was no word on any charges laid against the second man, but police said he was being held in relation to the same incident.
