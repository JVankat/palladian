Owner: Neuhoff Family

Web Site: http://www.kmvt.com/

History:
5/2004: Neuhoff Family buys KMVT from Catamount Broadcast Group for $17.3 million.

KMVT is a television station in Twin Falls, ID that serves the Twin Falls television market. The station runs programming from the CBS network. KMVT is a digital full-power television station that operates with 316 kilowatts of power and is owned by Neuhoff Family.
