Welcome to Gluten Free Voice!


GlutenFreeVoice.com was born out of a need to build a community of gluten-free persons like myself who understand what it means to be gluten free.
  • You never stop learning, researching and being that label detective.
  • You also never stop explaining your dietary needs more often than you'd like to more people than you'd like.
So often I've felt like a "freak" of nature because of the wide-eyed looks I'm met with when I tell people "yah, no regular bread...yah, no regular pasta, cookies, pies...nothing with wheat, barley, rye, some oats...blah. Ugh. It is as if I have an arm suddenly growing out of my forehead! One thing I have noticed is that I'm always excited when I meet someone else who is gluten free. We already know so much about one another because of the day to day struggle we share. It is like we've known one another for years! It is a funny thing. We need one other and a place where we can go to for sharing our experiences, our concerns, newest information, and of course recipes.

A place where we can go to get healthier and increase our day to day food satisfaction!
Band together gluten freaks! Let your gluten free voice be heard!
