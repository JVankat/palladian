By AMIE PARNES | 4/4/11 12:09 PM EDT Updated: 4/4/11 12:20 PM EDT

State dinner alert: President Obama and first lady Michelle Obama will host German Chancellor Angela Merkel for an official visit and state dinner in June, the White House announced on Monday.

The dinner, which will be Social Secretary Jeremy Bernard's first, will take place on June 7.

The last state dinner, in honor of Chinese President Hu Jintao, was in January.
