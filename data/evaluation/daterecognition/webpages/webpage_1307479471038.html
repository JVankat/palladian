Seattle Met Cover is here!

Check out the cover from my helicopter shoot for the Seattle Met magazine article “100 Reasons to Love Seattle,” which is just hitting the newsstands now.  Many thanks to Seattle Met art director Benjamen Purvis and to Egan Orion and his flash mob crew on the ground.  This issue was also just featured by SPD.org and Bobby Bonsey put together an excellent video using some of my video clips from the helicopter, very cool, thanks Bobby.
