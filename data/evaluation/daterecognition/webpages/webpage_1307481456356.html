Created: 05/01/09

Member id drchristy30 ( Feedback Score Of 21)

If you are taking the MCAT, you should DEFINITELY buy this book. It is very useful with showing you the kinds of problems that will be on the MCAT, the answers to those problems and WHY those are the answers, and the layout of the exam. The only thing I don't like about this book is that you only have 6 months of online access with it and 2 of the "3 exams included" are online. I wish it gave you unlimited access to their online program because it is very helpful.
OVERALL, BUY THIS BOOK IF YOU ARE TAKING THE MCAT.
