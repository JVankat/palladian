What is an Untour?

It's a way to live like a local when you travel: to see the sights and experience the lifestyle, with support when you want it and independence when you don't.

We provide the essentials - a unique place to stay in the form of a private vacation rental, help planning your flights, a way to get around once there, information on what to do, and a local contact if you need help. Unlike a guided tour, we don't tell you where to be and when, because it's your vacation. You're in control. And unlike a do-it-yourself vacation, you don't have to worry about finding the right accommodations or your best option for ground transportation. We've got you covered. Moreover, our experienced support staff is just a phone call away.

Here's how we do it:

  • We connect you to people: The dedicated staff who help you plan your Untour, the welcoming people who assist you during it, the locals you meet on your trip, and our enthusiastic clients all come together to make each Untour a delightful and meaningful vacation.
  • We connect you to a place: On an Untour, you unpack once and settle down to take in your surroundings. You savor food you could eat only there, laugh with people you could meet only there, and stroll a favorite street that could exist only there. When you return home, part of the place comes with you.
  • We connect you to the world: Untours is a green company that gives virtually all of its profits to people and companies that are making the world a better place. Your money goes to work while you go on vacation!
