Al-Anon
Help for families/friends of alcoholics, who share their experience, strength and hope to solve common problems. Seminyak Wed. 6:30 pm and Fri. 6:30 pm at the Dhyana Pura Hotel [Conference room area].

Aussie Rules Football
Players wanted, any standard to join the Quiksilver Bali Geckos for forthcoming matches. Call John 761165 or Jason 0812 380 2229.

BIWA
Bali- International Women Association (BIWA) is an association for Bali women from around the world. Every month we hold social meetings aimed at supporting several local charities directly. Come along to coffee mornings and lunches and meet other women and learn about what BIWA does and achieves and join in. Phone Margaret 281781 x 343 or Lian 287 482 or June 281 249.

Men's Club
Gay Men's Club soon to be opened in Bali. The only club in town that cares for men of all ages and national Kies. If you are lonely, need friends, tired of cruising around and no one cares - come and join us.
Do not hook on alcohol nor drugs. Feel the magic Loving Tender Care of Bali. Share your private concerns with other men in many kinds of positive activities. Call 082 836 0951 for enquiries. We offer strict confidentiality.

Sanur Residents
Join the Sanur project in working to make Sanur a better place to live, work, and visit. Recycling program, traffic reduction, an eco-friendly street festival, community education and more. Meetings second Thursday of every month at the Hotel Santai. Call 281 685 for more information.

Skat Club
German speaking Skat players meet for social contact, general discussion and having fun while playing Skat. Join us every Tuesday 8 pm at Villa Kresna - Seminyak. Call Fritz 730 317 for information.

Skal International
Bali Skal Club meets at noon, the first Friday each month. Call for meeting location, David Passman 753 037 or Debe 423 596. Skal International is a professional tourism industry association. Worldwide members and industry guests welcome.
