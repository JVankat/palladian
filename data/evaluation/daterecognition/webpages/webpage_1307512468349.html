APEH introduces simplified tax declarations

Friday 07:50, January 16th, 2009

  Hungary’s tax authority APEH is introducing a simplified tax disclosure opportunity for citizens in 2009, offering to calculate tax payment obligations for them, APEH Chairman János Szikora said.

Those taking advantage of APEH’s new service are to receive a declaration proposal by April 30, which – if accepted – is to be sent back signed to the authority by May 20. Szikora said APEH expects 250,000 to 1.5 million taxpayers to solicit the authority’s aid.

The only possible disadvantage to central processing is that tax refunds could take longer to be approved and transferred. Those tax subjects who choose to fill out and electronically submit their 2008 declarations could expect to receive their refunds starting February 1, Szikora added. (Napi Gazdaság)
