Car fails to stop after collision near Kendal

4:16pm Sunday 1st November 2009

  • Print
  • Email
  • Share
  • Comments(0)
  • By Ian Boydon »

POLICE are appealing for witnesses following a fail to stop road collision near Kendal.

A silver Toyota Yaris was damaged when it collided with a dark coloured car - described as having a loud exhaust - on Brettargh Holt roundabout, at around 6pm on Thursday.

The dark car left the roundabout onto the A591 heading towards Kendal.

Anyone with any information about this incident is urged to contact Cumbria Police on 0845 33 00 247.
