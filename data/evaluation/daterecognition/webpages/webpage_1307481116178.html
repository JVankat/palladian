Civic Engagement Programs

Pop the Bubble

Many students refer to the 'McMaster Bubble' as an imaginary boundary around the university campus. Students often find themselves spending large amounts of time on or around campus during their 4 years at McMaster while remaining isolated from the city that hosts them. Pop the Bubble encourages students to get off campus and into the Hamilton community. The McMaster Office of Community Service-Learning and Civic Engagement provides programs and resources to help students live, work and play their way to a more holistic and exciting student experience.

Questions? Concerns? Email us at popthebubble.mcmaster.ca or check out our blog for updates about exciting events and opportunities to pop your bubble!
