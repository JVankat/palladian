Ventura Attractions

With an incredible year-round average daytime temperature of 70 degrees, Ventura is a recreational paradise for any outdoor enthusiast. The beaches around Ventura are some of the most spectacular along the California coastline and provide limitless fun activities. Other things to do in Ventura include art walks, surfing, sailing, golfing, scuba diving, hiking, biking, and much more. Come stay at the Victoria Motel and enjoy the beautiful sites and fun activities that surround us.

Attractions near Victoria Motel:

 

  • Just off shore, the Channel Islands National Park is an all-natural playground for those interested in whale watching, hiking, diving, or kayaking through sea caves.
  • The golden beaches stretching from Surfer’s Point to Ventura Harbor are certainly a best kept secret – beautiful and warm with little or no crowds. This stretch includes surf breaks, a historic pier and promenade, San Buenaventura State Beach, the Seaward Avenue Beach District, several parks, miles of scenic bike paths, and a thriving marina.
  • Ventura Harbor has unique shopping, delicious restaurants and tons of entertainment for the entire family.
  • Ventura's famous ArtWalks are held twice a year in the historic downtown district.
  • As one of the world’s most famous areas for wine production, Ventura is home to many wineries for tours and wine tasting.
  • The Heritage Valley is close by where you’ll enjoy the California Oil Museum.
  • Ojai is nestled in the foothills of Los Padres National Forest and offers winery tours, romantic day spas and excellent golfing.
  • Enjoy the activities and family fun of the yearly Strawberry Festival in Oxnard, California, just 10 minutes south of Ventura.
