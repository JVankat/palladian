KEY RISK EXPANDS IN MARYLAND, OPENS BALTIMORE OFFICE
Triad -

HIRES 50 TO MANAGE WORKERS COMPENSATION SERVICES CONTRACT FOR CITY OF BALTIMORE


GREENSBORO � Key Risk, a leading provider of workers compensation products and services in the Eastern United States, today announced expansion of its operations to Maryland, as well as the establishment of new offices at the Wachovia Tower (Seven St. Paul Street) in Baltimore. The company is currently hiring 50 employees to provide third party administrative services for the recently awarded contract to manage workers compensation claims for the City of Baltimore.


�The award of the contract to manage workers compensation claims for the City of Baltimore marks a significant milestone in our Key Risk history,� said Rob Standen, President of Key Risk Management Services, Inc. �It signals our official expansion into Maryland and recognizes our distinctive expertise in working with municipal governments and public entities. 


Founded in 1986, Key Risk is a member company of W. R. Berkley Corporation, an insurance holding company that is among the largest commercial lines writers in the United States and operates five segments of the property casualty insurance business: specialty insurance, regional property casualty insurance, alternative markets, reinsurance and international.  Key Risk operates as an insurance company and third-party administrator from its headquarters in Greensboro, NC, and nine regional offices located in Atlanta, GA; Alpharetta, GA; Charlotte, NC; Columbia, SC; Nashville, TN; Raleigh, NC; Richmond, VA and Tampa, FL, in addition to the new office in Baltimore, MD.  For further information about the products and services available from Key Risk, please contact Anne Myers, Senior Vice President of Marketing & Client Services, at AMyers@KeyRisk.com.
