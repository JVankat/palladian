WELCOME TO THE COVENTRY VILLAGE LIFESTYLE

Coventry is known as a progressive neighborhood recognized for its incredible variety of entertainment, dining, and shopping.

Sometimes called Cleveland’s Greenwich Village, Coventry Village with its eclectic assortment of stores and restaurants is one of Cleveland area’s most original shopping experiences. Coventry has it all, it draws residents and tourists alike – whether you’re looking for a boutique, bustling pub, theater, fine dining experience, or just a cup of coffee, the Coventry neighborhood offers something for everyone.

In the last decade, young professionals have been increasingly attracted to this charming neighborhood, it is located next to University Circle (home to CASE, University Hospital, The Cleveland Clinic, The Botanical Gardens, The Western Reserve Historical Society, The Cleveland Orchestra and The Cleveland Museum of Art and Cleveland Natural History Museum) and Cleveland’s Famous “Little Italy” neighborhood; Coventry is also a short distance from downtown Cleveland.

The new, young demographic has also altered the Coventry landscape – “Rehab fever” has increased demand for older homes, and rejuvenated the area.
