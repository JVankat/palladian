Latest Quake Rumbles Norcal West Of Petrolia

A 4.0 magnitude earthquake was reported off the north coast of California at 8:10 a.m., about 8 miles west of the town of Petrolia and nearly 200 miles northwest of Sacramento. It was immediately followed by an aftershock of magnitude 2.4.

A Humboldt County sheriff's dispatcher says the agency has not gotten any injury or damage reports.
