Police investigate robbery at Hastings convenience store

Published: Thursday, August 19, 2010, 11:16 PM     Updated: Thursday, August 19, 2010, 11:17 PM
By Charles McChesney / The Post-Standard The Post-Standard
Follow
Tweet
Share close
Google Buzz Digg Stumble Upon Fark
Share Email Print

Hastings, NY -- State police are investigating a Wednesday night robbery at the Mobil Convenience Express on U.S. Route 11 in Hastings.

Police describe the suspect as a male between 5-foot-8-inches tall and 5-foot-10-inches tall, with a normal build, wearing a dark hooded sweatshirt, blue jeans and a white scarf covering his face.

No weapon was displayed and no threat was made, but the suspect demanded money when he entered the store.

The suspect fled on foot with money and cartons of cigarettes, police said.

No one was injured, police said.

Police ask that anyone with information on the robbery contact them at the Hastings barracks at 676-2214 or at the Pulaski barracks at 298-1444.
