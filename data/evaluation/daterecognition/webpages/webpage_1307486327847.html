The Microstar® Inlay Maker™

The Microstar® Inlay Maker™ is a revolutionary investing cylinder for extra control and precision when pressing inlays. Its oval cylinder provides a one-to-one relationship between the wax pattern and pressed inlay, permitting the divested ceramic to be seated with minimal effort. Inlay Maker™ also offers the ideal temperature and expansion during bench set, pre-heating, and pressing.

Available in 100g and 200g sizes.

Note: The Microstar Inlay Maker is designed to provide best results using HS-PC™ Investment, a very low expansion phosphate-bonded material specifically for inlays and onlays.
