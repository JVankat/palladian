Railway overbridges in and around Coimbatore demanded

    BJP Tamil Nadu unit urged Union Railway minister, Mamata Banerjee to take steps to construct railway overbridges at variaous level crossings at on the periphery of the city, to ease traffic jams.

    With traffic disruption becoming order of the day, the city and the citizens of this textile and pump and motors hub, were experiencing lot of hardship, particularly, when the railway gates were closed, BJP State secretary G K S Selvakumar said in a letter to Banerjee.

    Since the city was hosting a floating population of four to five lakh daily, from the neighbouring districts and States, it has become next to impossible to manage the vehicular traffic, leave alone pedestrians, particularly during the peak hours.

    As the Coimbatore division was giving high revenue to the Railways, the ministry should take immediate steps to construct overbridges in these places, for the benefit of the public, Selvakumar said.
