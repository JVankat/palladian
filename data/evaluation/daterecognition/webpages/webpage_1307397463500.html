Legal services

Legal services

Sorting out a mortgage is just the start. And with a host of other things to think about, why not share the load.

One of the things to consider is who will do the legal work involved in the buying or selling of your property?

If you're moving or buying your first home, we can offer a fixed cost conveyancing service on a 'no sale, no fee' basis. Our online quote system will give you an idea of the overall cost involved including disbursements.

Or, if you're remortgaging to us, there's just one fixed cost excluding compulsory disbursements.

We can also help you to get onto the property ladder with our Family Equity Scheme.

Either way, we can be of help as we have solicitors acting on our behalf to assist you with your legal needs.

Please note that the amount for disbursements and additional fees varies between England, Scotland, Wales and N. Ireland. You may wish to obtain an estimate before proceeding.

Remortgage, sale and/or purchase conveyancing service is provided by our approved panel conveyancer.
