Desktop Themes are a collection of graphics, pointers, sounds, startup/shutdown screens and screensavers provided to a common theme to enable you the user to change the way your desktop looks and sounds when in use. Microsoft sell a product called Plus!/Plus!98 - an add on to Windows 95/98 - which amongst other things allows you to do this easily and quickly. This has been a popular add-on as many thousands of Desktop Themes have been created on just about every subject you could imagine.
