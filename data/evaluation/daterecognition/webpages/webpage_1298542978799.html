Sailing The Whitsundays

Bareboat charter sailing in the Whitsundays is a sailor's dream. The channels are safe, calm and deep and the anchorages are protected and numerous. Yachts available for cruising at the Whitsundays consists of a world class line-up. There are modern cruising catamarans to berth yachts. Whereas, first-timers unsure of the waters can take advantage of a full briefing before they set out or a skipper will stay on board until they become more confident. But it isn't rocket science to skipper a Whitsunday bareboat, it's as simple as driving a car. Otherwise enjoy a fully skippered charter with professional yacht charter to sail the boat while you relax and enjoy the beautiful backdrop.

Fishing The Whitsundays

Fishing at the Whitsundays is amazing, it's as simple as casting your line out and reeling in a coral trout, a red emperor or sweetlip. For a more adventurous fishing experience, head out to the Great Barrier Reef on one of the fully equipped game fishing vessels available. Fishing in the all mysterious Great Barrier Reef, you can expect to catch anything from spanish mackeral to queenfish.

Sky-diving The Whitsundays

If you're looking for an adrenaline rush why not take off the The Great Barrier Reef by air or sea. Enjoy a scenic flight from a birds eye view in a helicopter, or dare to be dazzled by an outer reef adventure on aboard a seaplane. If you're looking for more of a rush, tandem skydiving and bungy jumping is also a popular and exhilarating Whitsunday activity.

Scuba Diving The Whitsundays

The Whitsundays is by far the best place to go diving, with thousands of people each year diving into its clear blue waters. You don't have to be an experienced diver to enjoy the beauty of the Whitsundays magnificent underwater realm. There are experienced dive operators who are trained to help first-timers have an unforgettable scuba diving experience. If you however are a more experienced diver, there are Whitsunday dive operators who are professionally trained to help you get the best out of your diving experience.

Whale Watching The Whitsundays

Experience the awe inspiring day out of watching the gentle giants of the sea, as they migrate their way along the Queensland coast to their calving grounds. This is a rare opportunity that will have you amazed and will open your eyes to one of the many wonders of the deep blue sea.
