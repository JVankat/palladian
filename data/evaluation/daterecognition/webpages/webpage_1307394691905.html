ABOUT US - Founded in ****, Munday & Collins AV is a pioneer in the audio-visual rental and production industry. In ****, the company decided to exit the "in house" audio-visual business to concentrate on meetings and events. By eliminating the costly overheard and commissions of the rental revenue typically charged by venues, Munday & Collins AV is focused on hiring experienced, service-oriented employees and technicians and acquired state-of-the art equipment to service the event industry. Since that time, Munday & Collins has helped companies and associations create high impact meetings and events at most of the largest venues in the West and maintains a high quality and experienced staff.

EQUIPMENT RENTAL - Reliable equipment is essential to a smooth-running event. Our Rental Department is the backbone of our business and directly supports our staging operations, trade show services and, meeting services. From large events to small corporate meetings, our staff can help you create quality presentations tailored to your specific needs. Contact us with your specific requirements.

SPECIAL EVENTS - Munday & Collins AV has played an integral role in countless corporate meetings and special events. Utilizing state-of-the- art equipment and an excellent staff of highly trained technical personel, our team is composed of audio and video specialists, cameramen and, lighting directors. Through the use of CAD renderings, we ensure the development of the most compatible system for each event.

Audio - The goal of our audio department is to create effective sound environments for each and every event. High performance sound systems with cutting-edge technology delivers words, music, and sounds customized to meet the needs of each specific audience.

Video - Munday & Collins AV has extensive video experience in seamless wide-screen, multi-screen, and multi-image presentations utilizing the latest projection, LED, and plasma display technologies. Leading-edge software allows wide-screen, multi-image video to be used for speaker support, image magnification and as a scenic element all at the same time.

  • High Bright LCD Projectors
  • High resolution, super bright DLP projectors
  • Component Production System
  • Wide-screen projection
  • Plasma Displays

    Lighting - Our lighting team consists of designers and project managers who have developed state-of-the-art lighting systems for a multitude of special, corporate, and entertainment events. We develop intricate CAD renderings, plotting out all aspects of the truss design, rigging, cable, and fixture placement. We provide an extensive inventory of conventional and moving lights, motors, truss, and leading edge control systems.

    Custom Staging and Sets - Munday & Collins AV designs high impact staging that get results. Through the use of modular set pieces and custom set designs, accommodating single and multiple screen requirements. We utilize our lighting resources and modular set pieces to produce everything from budget sets to high-end custom looks.

  • Modular and Custom Sets
  • Custom Design
  • Computer renderings
  • Specialty Drape
  • Scrims & Cycs
  • *-D Logo Signage

    MEETINGS & CONFERENCES - Munday & Collins AV specializes in the total coordination of audio visual and computer-networking requirements for major corporate and trade association meetings throughout the United States. We consult with our clients during the pre-planning and site inspection phase to insure that all equipment requirements and technical labor needs are carefully organized and on budget. Our goal is not only to meet our clients' needs, but also to anticipate their needs as they pertain to all service and equipment-related issues.

  • Pre-planning, site inspection consultation, and budget preparation
  • Assistance with room selection and equipment specifications
  • On-site event supervision
  • Detailed proposal and prompt billing after the meeting

    Representing many of the top manufacturers of audiovisual hardware, Munday & Collins' rental inventory is one of the nation's largest.

  • Daily, weekly, and monthly rental rates
