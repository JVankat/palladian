Many people want to start off each year with dietary changes as part of a weight loss program. It’s a good chance for a fresh start, so I like to help them make the right food choices and ensure that exercise is part of the overall regime. Something else that I’ve been recommended for the past five years, which has become the most important part of this program, is cleansing.

It’s one of the major – and often missing – components of a successful weight loss plan. I consider it one of the three keys to kick-starting a diet.

  1. Optimizing digestion: get a good cleanse supplement to help you get things “moving.”
  2. Managing your food intake: don’t just eat fewer calories, eat better calories – more fruits and vegetables, whole grains, and lean meats (try to eat organic foods when possible.)
  3. Becoming more active: Get out there and start moving. Make your exercise something you enjoy, and it’ll become part of your routine.

How does a cleanse optimize digestion?

A cleanse propels your body’s digestion system to help clear out the “junk” you may have accumulated over the holidays. With the right cleanse supplement, two weeks is about what it should take to get your body the momentum it needs to help you reach your fitness potential.

Not all cleanse supplements are alike.

Look for a good cleanse supplement – they’re not all the same. A good cleanse supplement program can give you the two-week “recharging” your health batteries need. The supplement should include:

  • Fiber to help pull out the bad and get things moving.You want to go with a fiber supplement that expands – but does so gently – in your intestines to scrub toxins and excess waste away from intestinal walls and helps prime your body for fitness. It doesn’t give the bad stuff any place to hide or a chance to “double back” and be re-absorbed. Oddly, many cleanse supplements don’t include fiber, so choose carefully.
  • A natural, gentle laxative formula.Look for a laxative formula that listens to the needs of your body. Make sure it’s a gentle, predictable laxative with no harsh stimulant ingredients to make you feel off balance – just botanicals that help get things “moving.”
  • Support for your liver, too.The liver is always busy. It helps the body keep the good and get rid of the bad. Using an herbal supplement that includes milk thistle can do an amazing job of supporting the liver’s ability to detoxify the body. One thing to look for when choosing a milk thistle product is that it is absorbable (look for products bound to phosphotidylcholine).

Used in conjunction with a healthy diet and exercise, a good cleanse really helps you jumpstart weight loss and energy to give you a fresh beginning in the New Year.
