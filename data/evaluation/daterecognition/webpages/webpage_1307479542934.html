2007 USCAA National Baseball Championship Tournament: Apprentice School Captures Title
Two-out ninth inning rally secures crown
May 10, 2007

Day One Recap   Day Two Recap   Day Three Recap

The following are capsule summaries of the final day of play in the 2007 USCAA National Baseball Championship Tournament. The Apprentice School captured the title.

Text continues below photo

GAME 9: Presentation 10, Florida College 9 Box Score
The Saints rallied from a 9-0 deficit in the bottom of the seventh inning to eliminate the Falcons in the third place game. Jarred Koopman was 3-for-5 for Presentation, while Kevin Mahoney and Logan Huber had two hits each. Three different Falcons had two hits apiece for Florida who ended their season 20-32.

GAME 10: Apprentice 4, Presentation 3 Box Score
A two-run single with two outs in the bottom of the ninth inning from James Denny scored Cato Dixon and J.D. Drewry as the Apprentice School defeated Presentation College for the 2007 USCAA National Championship. Freshman Aaron Penny came in and pitched two innings of relief to earn his third win of the year. Denny and Kyle Center were 2-for-4 apiece for the Builders. David Hermida and Zach Harry had three hits each for Presentation.

2007 All-Tournament Team

  • Catcher: Kyle Center, Apprentice School
  • Outfield: James Denny, Apprentice School; Mike Stinson, Florida College; Logan Huber, Presentation College
  • Infield: Robby Woodke, Florida College; Kevin Mahoney, Presentation College; John Rivera, Briarcliffe College; David Hermida, Presentation College
  • Pitcher: Mike Nelson, Apprentice School
  • MVP: Ryan Scott, Apprentice School
