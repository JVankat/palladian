Across the river from Washington DC, Crystal Gateway Marriott offers an ideal location for leisure vacations, business trips and weekend getaways. With a towering presence, this newly renovated Crystal City hotel offers exceptional accommodations, luxurious amenities and impeccable service. This Crystal City Marriott is surrounded by Arlington's best shopping and dining at the Crystal City shops and The Fashion Centre at Pentagon City. The Crystal City Metro stop can be accessed from the lobby of this Pentagon City hotel, allowing guests to reach the best museums, monuments and attractions of Washington DC and the surrounding area. Perfect for conferences, meetings and wedding receptions; the Crystal Gateway Marriott in Arlington, VA features banquet facilities and 27 meeting rooms, including a 16,000 square foot ballroom, totaling 34,300 square feet of meeting space. This comfortable and inviting hotel in Pentagon City is also conveniently located near Reagan National Airport. For GPS mapping use this address: 1601 South Eades Street. Location:
