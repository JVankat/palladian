Employment

Airex recognizes the value of a diverse employee base with a strong technological proficiency. We are interested in creative thinkers with a solid understanding of fundamental development work and high quality manufacturing activity.

We offer a competitive salary and benefit package to self-starting individuals who are interested in growth opportunities at Airex. Please click on the position that fits your qualifications to send a resume. We look forward to your interest in joining our dynamic development team.

 

  • ENGINEERING POSITIONS
    We have positions for electrical, mechanical, application and design engineers to play a major role in magnetic component design, development and manufacturing. You must be a team player that can excel in a self-motivating environment. Minimum qualifications include a BS/BA in Engineering and related experience.
  • CAD / DESIGNERS
    Designers/drafters with a working knowledge of 3D modeling work directly with Project Engineers in a fast paced environment. The position requires 3 to 5 years experience in Mechanical Desktop, Inventor and/or AutoCAD 2000. Some training will be provided.
  • TECHNICIANS
    This hands on position supports manufacturing activity and participates in prototype development efforts. The position requires 3 to 5 years experience in mechanical trades or electronic test and assembly.
  • MACHINIST / TOOLMAKER
    Experienced machinists/toolmakers fabricate assembly fixtures and molds in our model shop. Work from engineering prints or hand sketches in design/development and manufacturing support roles.
  • MECHANICAL ASSEMBLY / WINDING
    Entry-level position for the mechanically-inclined looking for new career opportunities. A solid work history and stability in previous employment is required.
  • MECHANICAL ASSEMBLY / MECHANICAL
    Entry-level position for the mechanically-inclined looking for new career opportunities. A solid work history and stability in previous employment is required.
  • OTHER POSITIONS
    Administrative positions are open periodically. Please submit appropriate resumes for consideration. Resumes are maintained on file for 12 months.
