Annual Summer Picnic Honoring Robert Doucette & George W. Curry

June 16, 2011

Time: 6.00pm to 9.00pm

 

The Everson Summer Picnic honors those who have been pillars of the community and have shaped Central New York through their gifts of leadership and strong conviction that art and creative expression enhance our quality of life. The Everson is proud to award the Everson Medal to Robert Doucette and George W. Curry, whose visionary work transformed the heart of Syracuse with the re-development of Armory Square, providing a platform for arts and culture to thrive downtown. 

Celebrate Mr. Doucette and Mr. Curry's contributions at the 2011 Everson Summer Picnic. Enjoy festive fare catered by The Sherwood Inn & Phoebe's, and live musical entertainment. Tickets are available for $200 per person. Tables, program ads, and sponsorship packages are also available. Please call (315) 474 6064 to make reservations.

60/60

June 17, 2011

Time: 5.30pm to 7.00pm

The Everson Members’ Council announces the third annual 60/60, back by popular demand. The event will take place under tents on the Everson Community Plaza surrounding the reflecting pools. Admission is $30 per person, $45 per patron, advance tickets are recommended.

Guests will enjoy a summer evening party all about the creation and appreciation of art. They will be invited to mingle and observe as 60 area artists create or finish original works of art in 60 minutes while enjoying refreshments.

A highlight of the evening will be a benefit drawing to win one of the amazing artworks being created live at the event. The raffle will be drawn at 6.30pm. The artworks are generously donated by participating artists.

This fun event goes by fast, so as a special thank you, those who order tickets online will be invited to begin registering early at 5.00pm the day of the event.

The Everson at Minnowbrook

October 16, 2011
October 17, 2011
October 18, 2011

Time: N/A

The Trip Committee is adding a special new destination to the itinerary this year. A two night stay at Minnowbrook Conference Center on scenic Blue Mountain Lake will delight participants with an engaging program, delightful accommodations, delicious dining and beautiful surroundings. This one-of-a-kind trip will take advantage of the natural beauty of the Adirondacks to spark conversations about art. Through the course of the trip, participants will have the opportunity to meet other members of the Everson Museum family, and hear behind the scenes details on the history of the Museum, its mission and future. This wonderful trip includes accommodations for two nights, 5 meals, happy hour beverages, all
lectures and discussions, and all tips.
