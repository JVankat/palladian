Limited liability joint-stock company UNDORIS JSC based in Klaipeda/Lithuania was founded in 2001. Over recent years, UNDORIS JSC has established itself as a reputed and reliable specialist in shiprepair and shipbuilding with extensive experience on Lithuanian, Scandinavian and EU markets.

Company aims to render prompt and reliable service to the Customers, without unnecessary bureaucracy.

We specialize in construction, repair, conversion, maintenance and modernization of shiphull and diverse maritime on&offshore structures, including welding, mounting, pipefitting, insulation, ventilation and related jobs.

Scope of services rendered by UNDORIS JSC:

  • ship building;
  • shiprepair;
  • construction and updating of artificial sea objects;
  • coastal steel constructions recycling;
  • sea objects and ships recycling;
  • manufacturing and repair of vessel hydraulic systems and mechanisms;
  • ship conversion and conversion works.
