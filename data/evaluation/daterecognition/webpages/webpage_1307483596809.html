November 20, 2009

This Weekend: Pavilions Day of Giving & Fashion Fusion

GO: Pavilions Day of Giving

{ Giving Is Receiving. }

What: A friends & family shopping event benefiting your charity of choice. Purchase a $20 Day of Giving Card to the event and enjoy 20% OFF discounts at select stores, wine & hors d’oeuvres, a chance to win a $1,000 Pavilions gift card, and donation of 100% of your ENTIRE card purchase to your charity of choice. More info.

When: Friday, November 20th; 10am-8pm

Where: The Pavilions; Sacramento.
……………………………………………………………………………
SUPPORT: Fashion Fusion

{ Strut Your Support. }

What: Join Couture Connection for Fashion Fusion, a charity fall fashion show & shopping event. Enjoy red carpet, paparazzi, champagne & hors d’ oeuvres with a couture fashion show main event & shopping afterparty. Percentage of purchases donated to the National MS Society. Tickets $15 in advance or at the door.  

When: Friday, November 20th; 7-11pm 

Where: Lounge ON20; 20th & K; midtown.
…………………………………………………………………………………….
GIVE: 2nd Annual Turkey Drive

{ Turkey Time. }

What: Join in helping the Sacramento Food Bank & Family Services and KCRA collect 3,000 turkeys to help local families in need this Thanksgiving. Can’t make it today? Donations of turkeys or other holiday fixin’s welcome through the end of the year.

When: Friday, November 20th; 4:30am-7pm

Where: Corner of 3rd Ave & 33rd Streets; Oak Park.
…………………………………………………………………………………….
ORDER: Thankgiving Pie Cake

{ Let Them Eat Pie. }

What: Surprise your Thanksgiving guests with a twist on the traditional dessert with a Frosted Cake Shop’s custom “pie” cake. Traditional or limited time “Fall” flavors available. 6″ carved pie $50; serves 10-12.

When: Order by Monday for Tues or Wed pick-up/delivery.

Where: www.thefrostedcakeshop.com; (916) 453.1981
………………………………………………………………………………………………………..
ENJOY: Midtown Ice Skating Rink

{ On Thin Ice. }

What: For the first time, join Midtown in hosting an ice skating rink during this winter season. Admission $8/adult, $5/children (12 and under), $2 skate rentals. Open 7 days a week; 10am-10pm. Holiday hours vary.  

When: Friday, November 20- January 18th.

Where: 20th Street between J & K; MARRS.
…………………………………………………………………………………………………………
PURCHASE: Beauty & Bubbles Tickets

{ Pampering For A Purpose. }

What: Join TwinSoup for a night of pampering to benefit Cure Breast Cancer Inc. Enjoy hair fashion show by Hoshall’s Salon & Spa, champagne and hors d’ oeurves, and exclusive TwinSoup Giveaways – all while helping to raise money to find a cure. Tickets $20 in advance. 21 years & up

When: Friday, December 4th; 6-9pm

Where: 10th & K Streets; Inside Social Night Club.
…………………………………………………………………………………………………
SIGN-UP: Holiday Cupcakes Cooking Class

{ Cupcakes 101. }

What: Learn how to make America’s favorite dessert with a Holiday twist at Ginger Elizabeth. Ginger will show you how to make, frost, and decorate fabulous festive cupcakes during this demonstration class. No handling, all tasting. Includes recipes to take home and dessert buffet. $35/person in advance.

When: Saturday, December 5th; 11am-1pm

Where: L Street Lofts Penthouse; 1818 L Street; midtown.
