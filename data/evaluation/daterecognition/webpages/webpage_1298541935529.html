Powell's Books presents Ruth Reichl

The Nines Hotel
Portland, OR


The editor-in-chief of "Gourmet" magazine, and a bestselling author in her own right, Ruth Reichl takes us on a behind the scenes look at "Gourmet Today."

All ticket holders receive a signed copy of the cookbook, "Gourmet Today," with ticket purchase. Books distributed at event.

This event takes place at The Nines Hotel 6th floor ballroom.
Cash bar reception - 7pm.
Ruth Reichl presentation - 7:30pm.

Note: ***We recommend you have tickets mailed to you to avoid the line at will-call.***
