Silhouette was founded by Anneliese and Arnold Schmied of Austria in 1964; they had one idea in mind, to transfer eyeglasses into a coveted and beautiful accessory. Their commitment to the aesthetics of rimless eyewear that brings out the personality of the wearer could be a milestone in the history of eyewear in the 21st century. In 1983, Silhouette created and developed the first rimless eyewear collection: Silhouette Freeline. It triggered an endless fascination with rimless eyewear and a passionate dedication to the genre and the spirit of innovation. The Silhouette rimless eyeglasses collection stands for essential, extravagant and luxury lifestyle that is loved by many around the world.
